iOS BETA Install Instructions

Approve the Developer

  1. Download the app from this page
  2. On your Phone or Tablet, goto:
    Settings > General > Profiles or Profiles & Device Management.
  3. Under the "Enterprise App" heading, you see a profile for the Activewebsite LLC.
  4. Tap Activewebsite LLC heading to establish trust for this developer.
  5. You should then you see a prompt to confirm your choice
  6. Now go to your homescreen and launch the app!
